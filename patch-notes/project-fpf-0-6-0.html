0.6.0 Patch Notes - (Blitz Mode)

October 24, 2023


Note: Not all notes are present and there are notes that may not be worked on in the future

Previous Patch Notes Next Patch Notes

Card Updates:

  • Adjusted Obsetileo
    • Old: Has 3 points
    • New: Has 4 points
    • Notes: This was to make Obsetileo a bit stronger compared to Chronos who had a similar but stronger effect at the same points.
  • Adjusted Shop Owner
    • Old: Has 1 point
    • New: Has 0 points
    • Notes: Shop Owner would Boost itself so it would become 3-points plus the additional Boosts it gives.
  • Adjusted Citizen
    • Old: KO: Give all squares Boost -3
    • New: KO: Give all squares Boost -2
    • Notes: Destroying Citizen is a devastating move on the board, so tuning it down should be enough
  • Adjusted The Shadow
    • Old: Has -3 point
    • New: Has -2 points
    • Notes: The Light that The Shadow generates didn’t feel much of a benefit for playing The Shadow.

NEW:

  • Keywords now show what they mean in deck-builder and in-game
  • Added card viewer for cards in your deck while editing in the collection
  • Added Blitz, where the game ends after Round 4 and the events change on Round 3.

Updates:

  • Viewing cards in-game now show the card that is associated with them
  • Removed Hawaiian Festival event as it is undergoing bug fixes.

Bug Fixes:

  • Fixed Buffering event to properly change to a new event after 1 round
  • Hero-In-Training is now in the Ilia class as it wasn’t before
  • Adjusted UI to fit exactly 8 decks when selecting one in the lobby
  • In the deck-builder, whenever an Elite card is removed from the deck, the collection will update and the player must select a new Elite card
  • In the deck-builder, class cards will also be removed from the deck if the Elite card is removed
