0.7.2 Patch Notes - (Deck Balancing)

December 12, 2023


Note: Not all notes are present and there are notes that may not be worked on in the future

Previous Patch Notes Next Patch Notes

Card Updates:

  • All class cards are now in their respective classes
  • Added Ivina Recruiter
    • Points: 1
    • On-Summon: Add a random card to your hand
  • Added Radium Scientist
    • Points: 5
    • Burst:: Give this square Boost -3

The following cards are changed to further balance the game.

  • Adjusted Chronos - Future
    • Old: Post-Phase: Give this square Boost +1
    • New: Post-Phase: Give this square Boost +1 and a random enemy square Boost -1
  • Adjusted Chronos - Past
    • Old: Pre-Phase: Give this square Boost +1
    • New: Pre-Phase: Give this square and a random ally square Boost +1
  • Adjusted Temperal Particle
    • Old: On-Summon: Give this square Boost +1
    • New: Burst: Remove all Boosts from a random enemy square
  • Adjusted Ilia, The Huntress
    • Old: Had 4 points | On-Summon: Destroy a random enemy summon
    • New: Has 1 points | On-Summon: Destroy the enemy summon with the highest points
  • Adjusted Personal Mentor
    • Old: Had 6 points | Burst: Destroy this and a random enemy summon
    • New: Has 2 points | Delay: (2) Burst: Destroy a random enemy summon
  • Adjusted Weapon Repairsman
    • Old: Had 5 points | You can only play this if you own at least 1 summon Burst: Destroy a random friendly summon
    • New: Has 3 points | You can only play this if you own at least 1 summon Burst: Destroy the friendly summon with lowest points
  • Adjusted Citizen
    • Old: Had 1 Point
    • New: Had 2 Points
  • Adjusted Shop Owner
    • Old: Burst: Give all summons you control +2 Points
    • New: Burst: Give all summons you control Boost +2
  • Adjusted Great Elephant
    • Old: On-Summon: Give all adjacent squares Boost +0
    • New: Burst: Give all adjacent summons allies +2 points and adjacent enemies Boost -2
  • Adjusted Fire Thrower
    • Old: Aura: All summons with no Boost gain +2 Points
    • New: Burst: Give all summons you control +2 Points
  • Adjusted Metal Knight
    • Old: Had 3 Point
    • New: Had 4 Points
  • Adjusted Young Squire
    • Old: Aura: If this square has no Boost gain +2 Points
    • New: Aura: If this square has no Boost gain +5 Points
  • Adjusted Lynx
    • Old: Obsetileo class
    • New: Neutral class
  • Adjusted Shieldbearer
    • Old: Separona class | Delay: (2) Aura: Cannot be destroyed
    • New: Obsetileo class | Aura: Cannot be destroyed
  • Adjusted Separona
    • Old: Had 1 Point
    • New: Had 2 Points
  • Adjusted Hammer Smith
    • Old: On-Summon: Give a random adjacent square of this Boost +1
    • New: On-Summon: Give a random enemy summon Boost -1
  • Adjusted Trainee
    • Old: KO: Summon a Trainee onto a random square
    • New: On-Summon: Give this square Boost +1 KO: Summon a Trainee onto a random square
  • Adjusted Professional Trainer
    • Old: Neutral class | On-Summon and KO: Summon a Trainee onto a random square
    • New: Separona class | Sidekick: Trainee KO: Summon a Trainee onto a random square

NEW:

  • Added “Hot events” that have a 20% chance of appearing in matches.
  • Added “Bots” for players who can’t find an opponent.

Updates:

  • Reworked the keywords and conditions of cards and events, but still functionally worked the same.
  • Private games now have waiting rooms similar to searching for a random match.

Bug Fixes:

  • Fixed the bug where the event Apocalypse activated twice.
  • Fixed a bug where the event Fall Migration and City Parade would spawn summons on the same square.
  • Fixed the bug where square were showing incorrect owners from Aura effects
