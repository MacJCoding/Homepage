0.5.0 Patch Notes - (Ilia and Events)

September 2, 2023


Note: Not all notes are present and there are notes that may not be worked on in the future

Previous Patch Notes Next Patch Notes

Card Updates:

  • Added Ilia, The Huntress
    • Points: 4
    • On-Summon: Destroy a random enemy summon
  • Added Personal Mentor
    • Points: 6
    • Burst: Destroy this and a random enemy summon
  • Added Weapon Repairsman
    • Points: 5
    • You can only play this if you control at least 1 summon Burst: Destroy a random friendly summon
  • Added Insider Spy
    • Points: 0
    • On-Summon: Give a random enemy summon -2 Points
  • Added Hero-In-Training
    • Points: 2
    • Aura: Whenever you Burst, destroy all adjecent summons from this
  • Added Archer Trainer
    • Points: 1
    • On-Summon: Give 4 random squares Boost +2
  • Added Citizen
    • Points: 1
    • KO: Give all squares Boost -3
  • Added Criminal
    • Points: -2
    • KO: Give all squares you control Boost +3
  • Added Shop Owner
    • Points: 1
    • Burst: Give all summons you control +2 Points
  • Added Aiki - The Space Guardian
    • Points: 2
    • Burst: Change the current event
  • Added Dark Separona
    • Points: 7
    • You can only play this if you control at least 3 summons On-Summon: Destroy all your other summons
  • Added The Living Mountain
    • Points: 10
    • Delay: (6)
  • Adjusted Shieldbearer
    • Old: Aura: This cannot be destroyed
    • New: Delay: (2) Aura: This cannot be destroyed
    • Notes: This was to tune Shieldbearer down a bit to counter its very strong aura effect.

NEW:

  • Added new Mechanic - Delay
    • Delay: (x) means a card is unplayable in hand until x reaches zero. At the start of each turn, decrease x by 1.
  • Added Ilia, The Huntress Preset Deck
  • Added Events that put into play twice per game
    • Apocalypse
      • Destroy all summons
    • Buffering
      • Change to a NEW event next round
    • Burrow Malfunction
      • Add Aiki - The Space Guardian to each player's hand
    • City Parade
      • Summon a Criminal and Citizen for both players
    • Explosion at Great Towers of Parjing
      • Remove all Boosts from all squares
    • Fall Migration
      • Summon 3 Pigeons for both players
    • Gentle Breeze
      • Give all summons currently in play Boost +1
    • Hawaiian Festival
      • All Burst effects don't trigger
    • Ivina Tournament
      • Shuffle 3 Elite cards to both players deck
    • Landslide
      • Summon The Living Mountain for both players
    • Lockdown
      • Summon Shieldbearer for both players
    • Metalwork Commission
      • Summons on squares with no Boosts have +3 Power
    • Ribben Rose Invitational
      • At the end of your turn, give your summons Boost +1
    • Ripple In Time
      • The game ends after this round
    • Romdon Festival
      • Fill both players' hand with random cards
    • Spring Migration
      • At the end of your turn, add a Pigeon to your hand
    • Sunny Day
      • Clear Skies
    • Town Square
      • Replace both players decks with 10 random cards
    • Tsunami
      • Both players draws 3 cards and their deck is washed away

Updates:

  • Cards in the collection now preview cards that are associated with them
  • Deck building now starts with selecting an Elite card before choosing other cards
  • Added animations delays in cards are summoned
  • Changed in-game UI to where players can view the cards by right-clicking the card or summon. This is to avoid cluttering the gameplay.
  • Changed the End Turn button only to appear if the player cannot play a card at all, thus players must play at least one card per turn.
  • Squares on the board now change colors if the collective Boosts on it is either positive (green) or negative (yellow)
  • Added points now show where they originated from - other summons, auras, or events
  • Summons now show their points in red if it is below the summons’ original points
  • Total points now show on the side of the board rather than near the players’ names
  • Changed Lobby UI to add “more flare”

Bug Fixes:

  • Fixed issues when lobby would snap to appear rather than with transition
  • Metal Knight now works as intended
  • Players now have the intended max hand size of 7
