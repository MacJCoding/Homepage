What is Fusara Duelists?

Project Fusara Duelists is a rogue-like deckbuilding card game in the making. There are many creatures that lurk in the dungeons of Fusara Duelists. As your selected hero, you must traverse through the dungeons as far as possible. You must duel against these creatures with your special crafted deck that you can edit along your journey. Good luck, hero, and may the power of the Fusara be upon you.

Meet the Heroes

You must select a hero that you will play as before you enter the dungeons. You can choose any of the heroes below:

Obsetileo

Obsetileo is warrior with blazing strategy. He focuses on his fireballs and fire novas for damage, but he is quite slow for attacks. His armor-related cards will allow you to survive attacks, so that you can find an opening and get the killing blow.
