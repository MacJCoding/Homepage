What is Project FPF?

Project FPF is a stategic deck-building card game in the making. Play against other players to claim the most points by the end of the game. Use the abilities of many characters and your elites, to turn the game in your favor.

Meet the Elites

Each deck must start with one Elite, and each Elite mostly determines your deck strategy

There are many Elites in each class. Here are the major Elites for each one:

Obsetileo

Obsetileo is warrior with blazing strategy. He focuses on his fireballs and fire novas for damage, but he is quite slow for attacks. His armor-related cards will allow you to survive attacks, so that you can find an opening and get the killing blow.
